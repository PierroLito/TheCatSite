Why Cats Are Amazing

Cats are one of the most popular pets worldwide. They are known for their independence, grace, and playful nature. Whether you’re a lifelong cat lover or a new pet owner, there’s always more to learn about these fascinating creatures.

Fun Facts About Cats

  • Cats sleep 12-16 hours a day.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four on the back ones.
